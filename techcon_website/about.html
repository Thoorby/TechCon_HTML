Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech innovators passionate about exploring the future of software engineering, AI, and emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of attendees across various industries.

The conference has evolved from a single-day event into a weeklong experience filled with workshops, keynote speeches, technology showcases, and networking opportunities.
